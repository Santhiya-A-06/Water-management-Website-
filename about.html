Our Mission

To create a transparent, accountable, and collaborative ecosystem for water quality management.

What is CleanWater360?

CleanWater360 is a citizen-centric platform designed to enhance public health and environmental safety. We provide the tools for communities to report water quality issues, track government response, and access vital safety information in real-time.

How It Works

1. Observe

Citizens notice issues like unusual color, smell, or taste in their water supply.

2. Report

Upload details and photos securely through our platform. We automatically geotag the location.

3. Resolve

Authorities triage the issue and deploy teams. You get notified when it's fixed.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, CleanWater360 emerged from a simple idea: that every citizen should have the power to protect their local water sources. What started as a small community project has grown into a nationwide platform connecting thousands of citizens with water management authorities.

We believe that data transparency is key to accountability. By making water quality data accessible to everyone, we empower communities to demand better standards and cleaner water.
